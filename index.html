Ethics and Pricing for WordPress Agencies

In this episode of The WP Minute+ podcast, Kurt and Toby delve into the ethical and pricing challenges faced by WordPress agencies. They discuss the importance of valuing expertise over cheaper alternatives, maintaining ethical standards in client relationships, and adapting pricing models to reflect service value. Key takeaways include the significance of discipline, the evolving role of SEO, and the need for agencies to attract the right clients through strategic marketing.

Top 10 Ways for Hosting Companies to Reach (And Keep) New Customers

The article outlines ten effective strategies for WordPress hosting companies to attract and retain customers. Key tactics include newsletter and podcast sponsorships, YouTube channel sponsorships, guest spots on podcasts, WordCamp sponsorships, guest articles, customer surveys, social media outreach, attending industry events, and hosting webinars or office hours. The emphasis is on building trust, leveraging community engagement, and providing value to both new and existing customers.

Why Sponsoring WordPress Content Creators is a Win for Your Brand

The article highlights the strategic value of sponsoring WordPress content creators, such as bloggers, YouTubers, and podcast hosts, alongside traditional developer sponsorships. It emphasizes how these creators amplify brand visibility, drive product adoption, and foster community trust through evergreen educational content. By investing in educators, companies not only enhance user onboarding but also contribute to the long-term growth and sustainability of the WordPress ecosystem. The piece underscores the complementary roles of developer and educator sponsorships, advocating for a balanced approach to maximize impact.
