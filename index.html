Do Founders Make Great Marketers?

This podcast episode features Ben Pines discussing founder-led marketing in the WordPress ecosystem. Ben shares his journey from being an early Elementor employee to starting his own consulting business, focusing on the unique challenges business owners face in marketing their products. The key insights include the importance of building authority, the changing effectiveness of SEO for plugin marketing, and the need for strategic approaches to monetization and distribution. Ben emphasizes that founder-led marketing requires a specific mindset, balancing human connection with scalability, and highlights the crucial role of community engagement and internal motivation for business success.

Is Your Agency Focused on the Right Things?

This Agency Action podcast episode features Kurt and Toby discussing strategic focus for WordPress agencies. They explore the importance of continually reassessing business priorities, balancing work with family life, and navigating different client expectations when working with larger corporate clients versus smaller businesses. The conversation covers effective sales strategies that emphasize clarity and direct communication, the value of building strong business relationships through networking, and finding the right balance between design aesthetics and functional value. Key insights include how smaller projects can slow progress toward larger goals, the complexities of corporate work involving multiple stakeholders, and the need to ensure design doesn't overshadow the core service value.
