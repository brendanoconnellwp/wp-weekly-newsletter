The Essential WordPress Stack for Agencies

This Agency Action podcast episode features Kurt von Ahnen and Toby Cryns sharing their WordPress agency expertise, focusing on the essential technology stack and operational strategies for successful agency management. They discuss critical components including plugin selection, hosting providers, backup solutions, and community-building tools like Lifter LMS, emphasizing the importance of filtering plugins and maintaining independent backup systems separate from hosting services. The conversation covers broader business management aspects such as effective team dynamics, project delivery methods, and cost management strategies. Key insights include balancing frugality with efficiency, using tools like Descript and SweetDash to streamline operations, and the importance of transparency in business finances. The hosts also address proposal writing, client communication, time tracking for better project estimates, and the necessity of business insurance for agency sustainability and risk management.

WP Accessibility Day Is a Learning Opportunity for All

This podcast episode from The WP Minute features Eric Karkovack discussing web accessibility with experts Adrienne Grace and Isla Waite, focusing on the upcoming WP Accessibility Day (October 15-16, 2025). The conversation emphasizes that web accessibility is both a moral and legal obligation, highlighting common issues like color contrast problems and the importance of proper form labels beyond placeholder text. The experts stress that accessibility should be integrated from the beginning of the design process and that inclusive design benefits all users, not just those with disabilities. The discussion addresses practical challenges such as finding accessible themes and plugins, and the limitations of AI in accessibility testing, noting that human oversight remains essential. Key recommendations include using accessibility checker plugins, providing client education for maintaining accessible websites, and leveraging events like WP Accessibility Day for ongoing learning. The episode underscores that proper accessibility education leads to better business outcomes and more inclusive web experiences for everyone.
