Emotionally Unemployable: Agency Life with Kurt and Toby

In this episode of The WP Minute+, Kurt von Ahnen and Toby Cryns share their experiences and insights on running a small WordPress agency. They discuss the challenges and rewards of autonomy, client management, and niche expertise, emphasizing the importance of community engagement and adaptability. Key takeaways include strategies like letting go of low-value clients, tracking project profitability through effective labor rates, and offering productized services for sustainable income. The hosts also highlight the role of personal connections and industry familiarity in sales success.

What Do AI Site Builders Mean for Freelancers?

The article explores the rise of AI site builders and their potential impact on freelance web designers. While these tools offer quick, budget-friendly solutions for DIY users, they currently lack the sophistication of custom builds. The technology is expected to improve, but its limitations suggest freelancers will still be needed for complex projects and personalized client relationships. The key for freelancers is to position themselves as experts offering tailored solutions and superior customer service, which AI cannot replicate.
