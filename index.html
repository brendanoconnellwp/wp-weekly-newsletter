The Future of WordPress With Mary Hubbard

This article features a podcast interview with Mary Hubbard, the Executive Director of WordPress, discussing her first year in the role and the future direction of the platform. Key topics include WordPress 7.0 development with AI integration, improved media handling, and responsive design controls, as well as community engagement strategies, non-coding contributions, and educational programs to attract new contributors. WordPress in Your Browser? How my.WordPress.net Makes the CMS Personal

This article explores my.WordPress.net, a new browser-based WordPress sandbox that transforms the traditional CMS into a private, portable workspace. Built on WordPress Playground technology, this innovative project emphasizes privacy, portability, and AI integration while enabling users to create personal applications like contact management systems, private blogs, and experimental AI playgrounds. The platform rebrands plugins as app-like modules for tailored functionality and serves as a sandbox for experimentation before production deployment. Key features include AI integration for content creation and automation, with future plans for device synchronization and potential applications in private social networks and personal dashboards. The project aims to lower barriers for non-developers and foster community innovation with new WordPress-based applications.

How Better Communication Puts Your Agency In Control

This article features a podcast discussion on the critical role of effective communication in running a successful agency. The conversation explores strategies for setting clear expectations, handling unknowns, and leading clients confidently while fostering team growth. Key communication approaches include establishing hard deadlines, controlling project narratives, and practicing deep listening to eliminate scope creep and reduce client frustration. The discussion emphasizes that transparency serves as a foundation for exponential agency growth, with practical tactics for managing client relationships through regular "heartbeat" updates and balancing information sharing. Additional insights cover running effective meetings with structured agendas, developing team soft skills, and using open-ended questions to prevent misunderstandings in demanding situations.
