A Sneak Peek at Pressable's Plans for 2026

This article covers a podcast interview with Kevin MacGillivray, Pressable's Chief Marketing Officer, discussing the company's strategic direction for 2026. Key topics include his transition from Shopify to the WordPress ecosystem, the challenges of marketing in a flexible environment, and how AI is transforming marketing strategies and product interactions. Pressable is focusing on building strong agency partnerships by providing seamless experiences and engaging in co-marketing initiatives. The company emphasizes community building for knowledge sharing and growth, plans to expand its presence at industry events, and stresses that trust is built through human connections rather than automation. Differentiation in product marketing and targeted storytelling with agencies are central to Pressable's 2026 strategy.

Agency Owners: Stop Doing It All Yourself

This podcast episode from The WP Minute's Agency Action series features Kurt and Toby discussing the critical importance of delegation and strategic hiring for agency growth. They explore how agency owners often hinder their own progress by trying to handle everything themselves, and emphasize the need to let go of control to achieve better outcomes. The conversation covers ethical challenges in agency work, the importance of understanding pricing and value, and how automation can significantly improve operational efficiency. Key insights include the necessity of strategic hiring to drive growth, the importance of investing in your business for long-term viability, and embracing the entrepreneurial mindset required to build a sustainable agency.
